Privacy Policy for LiteLight

Effective Date: April 19, 2026

LiteLight ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that LiteLight does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users.

1. No Data Collection

LiteLight does not collect, use, save, or share any personal information. The app functions entirely on your device without transmitting any data to external servers.

2. Permissions Used

LiteLight requires the following permissions solely for core functionality:

  • Camera Permission: Required to control the device's flashlight LED. The camera is never used to take photos or record video.

These permissions are used only for the flashlight feature and no other purpose.

3. Third-Party Services

LiteLight uses no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting services. All functionality is self-contained on your device.

4. Data Security

Since LiteLight collects no data, there is no data to be compromised. The app operates entirely offline with no internet permission required.

5. Children's Privacy

LiteLight does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app contains no interactive features, advertising, or data collection mechanisms.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
